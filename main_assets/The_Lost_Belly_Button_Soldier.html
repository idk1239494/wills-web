The Lost Belly Button Soldier is a belly button soldier that was allegedly left behind by the rest during a battle somewhere in the late 20th century. A while later in 2007, photos began surfacing online of apparent sightings of the belly button soldier.

Backstory

Belly button soldiers were types of soldiers that put their shirt over their head, causing it to get pulled up and showing their belly button. They never wore shoes but they had to wear the assigned uniform, royal red and gold shirt and green leggings. The Lost Belly Button Soldier was also a belly button soldier who fought in every battle. Allegedly, the Lost Belly Button Soldier left behind during the battle of Shirts and Footwear of 1988. It is believed that they were captured by the enemy soldiers and was held captive for 8 years. Eventually, the belly button soldier could take no more and murdered almost all of the soldiers keeping them captured. Allegedly, the weapons they had did not work against the belly button soldier. The belly button soldier ended up escaping the isolated facility, into the surrounding forest, and is now the only belly button soldier left. They then will be forgotten for 19 years, until one day..
